Gordon Andrews catalogues

These rare surviving catalogues and invitation were designed by Gordon Andrews who designed numerous David Jones gallery and other exhibition catalogues during the late 1940s.

Australian Decimal Banknotes (1966)

Gordon Andrews designed Australia’s first decimal banknotes, introduced in 1966, creating a distinctly Australian visual identity. The $2 note features John Macarthur, a key figure in the wool industry, with intricate security patterns and vibrant colours. Andrews’ work combined artistic detail with anti-counterfeiting measures, shaping the future of Australian currency design.

Furniture Design (1950s–1970s)

Andrews was an active furniture designer, producing a range of modernist chairs, tables, and storage units. His furniture designs reflected his commitment to simplicity, function, and refined aesthetics.
